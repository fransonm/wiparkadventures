Park Description

Buckhorn State Park is in central Wisconsin on Castle Rock Lake on the Wisconsin River. There are many hiking trails and walk-in camping sites within the park.

During my visit to the park, I hiked only the Central Sands Natrue Trail. It is just under a mile and a half loop trail that can be accessed from the parking area labeled "access for sites #17-#19" or from Hunter Parking Lot A. There were many different kinds of flowers growing the prairie grass.
