My Mission

I am on a mission to visit all of the state parks in Wisconsin. I love spending time in the great outdoors and enjoying the beauty of nature. We are lucky to have many great outdoor areas open to public recreational use in Wisconsin, and I am bound and determined to see them all.

Wisconsin pubilc lands are grouped into categories depending on how they are is used and managed: parks, forests, wildlife areas, fisheries areas, natural areas, trails, and flowages

For now, I am starting with just three of these: parks, trails, and wildlife areas. I will be adding new pictures and information as I visit new areas.
